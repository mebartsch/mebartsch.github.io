The World of Aaapolopolis

Below are some images and captions to explain the different aspects of Aaapolopolis.

This is the flag all Aaapolopians respect and honor. When we say the pledge, Aaapolopians put their right hand behind their backs and their left hand should be dropped to their side. The three As on the flag represent the first three founders of this country. The sun signifies, that no matter what mistakes were made yesterday, there is always a new day and the sun will keep on rising and setting.

The Aaapolopolis Pledge 

		I pledge to Aaapolopolis with all my heart.
		It gives me a home when I'm in the dark.
		If war comes by, I fight to the end.
		We will stay strong, 'cause united we stand.
This is a salt dough map of Aaapolopolis. It shows our Apple Delta, mountains, lakes, islands and our volcano, Mt. Ginnabob.

This map shows the names of all our cities, mountain ranges, lakes, and volcanoes.

This letter, typed on a typewriter, explains the different types of music in Aaapolopolis and how instruments are made through the perspective of a young girl.

This is the currency of Aaapolopolis. Each swirl represents one more in the tens place if there is a hexagon, and one more in the ones place if there is not. The hexagon acts as a zero in the ones place.

This is a travel poster advertising our amazing apple pie, the coral reef in Aaapolopolis, and our volcano.
